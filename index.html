Help Menu

Camera Controls

  • Hold left click and drag mouse to rotate the cube
  • Scroll down to zoom out
  • Scroll up to zoom in

Cube Controls

  • Press space to scramble the cube
  • Press enter to solve the cube

Animation / Audio Controls

  • Press Arrow Up to increase the animation time (Slow down cube)
  • Press Arrow Down to decrease the animation time (Speed up cube)
  • Press a to toggle animation
  • Press m to toggle audio
